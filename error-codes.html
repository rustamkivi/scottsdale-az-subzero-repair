Common Error Codes

Sub-Zero Error Codes — What They Mean

We fix all Sub-Zero error codes same day across Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, and Fountain Hills.

EC

Evaporator Coil Sensor Error

Temperature sensor on evaporator coil has failed or is reading incorrectly. Common after Arizona summer heat stress.

✓ Sensor replaced same day
HI TEMP

High Temperature Alarm

Internal temperature exceeded safe range. Food at risk. Can be caused by door left open, failed fan, or cooling system issue.

✓ Emergency same-day priority
FC

Freezer Sensor Error

Freezer compartment temperature sensor failure. Unit can't regulate freezer temperature properly.

✓ Same-day diagnosis and fix
ALARM

Door Ajar Alarm

Door not sealing properly — worn gasket or misaligned door. Warm Arizona air enters and raises internal temperature.

✓ Gasket or alignment fixed
PF

Power Failure Alarm

Sub-Zero detected a power interruption. Often appears after Arizona monsoon storms. Usually clears itself — call us if it persists.

✓ Diagnosed same day
SVC

Service Required

Generic service alert — Sub-Zero detected an issue requiring technician diagnosis. Don't ignore this code.

✓ Full diagnosis same day
