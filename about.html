Our Story

9+ Years of Sub-Zero Experience

We started with a simple observation: most appliance repair companies treat Sub-Zero like any other refrigerator. They show up with generic parts, generic knowledge, and generic results. Sub-Zero deserves better.

With 9+ years focused exclusively on premium appliances, we've seen every failure mode Sub-Zero units develop — especially in Arizona's extreme climate. Scottsdale's 115°F summers and dust storms create problems you simply don't see in other markets.

We built our entire operation around this: same-day response, parts stocked on the truck, upfront pricing, and a 12-month warranty on every repair. No surprises, no runaround.

Our clients are Scottsdale homeowners who invested $8,000–$15,000 in a Sub-Zero. They deserve a technician who treats that investment with the respect it deserves.
