Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before calling.

Do you service all Sub-Zero models?
Yes — all Sub-Zero models including built-in refrigerators, integrated units, undercounter fridges, wine storage, and the newer Designer and Classic series.
My Sub-Zero is not cooling — how fast can you come?
Call us and in most cases we arrive the same day. Food safety is urgent — we prioritize "not cooling" calls across all Scottsdale zip codes and Paradise Valley.
Ice maker stopped working — is it worth repairing?
Almost always yes. Sub-Zero ice maker issues are usually caused by a failed water valve, ice maker module, or clogged filter — all straightforward repairs that cost far less than replacement.
What does the 12-month warranty cover?
All labor and parts installed during the repair. Same issue returns within 12 months — we come back and fix it at no charge. Provided in writing.
Is the diagnostic fee free?
Yes — diagnostic visit is free when you proceed with the repair. We find the issue, give you the price, and only charge if you approve.
Is Sub-Zero repair worth it vs. replacing?
Sub-Zero refrigerators are built to last 20+ years. A repair at a fraction of replacement cost — with a 12-month warranty — almost always makes financial sense for Scottsdale homeowners.
