FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before calling.

How fast can you come to Scottsdale?
Same day in most cases. Call before noon and we typically arrive that afternoon. We serve all Scottsdale zip codes, Paradise Valley 85253, Fountain Hills 85268, and North Scottsdale.
Does Arizona heat affect my Sub-Zero?
Yes — significantly. At 115°F outside, your Sub-Zero works 3× harder than in cooler climates. Arizona dust also clogs condenser coils much faster. This is the most common reason Sub-Zero units fail in Scottsdale.
Is it worth repairing vs. buying new?
Almost always yes. A new Sub-Zero costs $8,000–$15,000. Most repairs run $200–$600. With our 12-month warranty, repair makes financial sense in nearly every case.
What does the $89 service call include?
The $89 covers the technician visit and full diagnosis. If you proceed with the repair, it applies toward the total. You know the exact cost before we start — no surprises.
What Sub-Zero models do you service?
All of them — Pro 48, Integrated, Classic, Designer Series, Column units, Undercounter, Wine Storage, Drawer refrigerators, and all Built-In models.
Do you carry parts on the truck?
Yes — we stock the most common Sub-Zero parts. Most repairs are completed same day on the first visit without waiting for parts to arrive.
