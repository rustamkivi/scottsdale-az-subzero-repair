Why It Stopped Working

Common Causes of Sub-Zero Ice Maker Failure

We diagnose the exact cause on the first visit and fix it same day in most cases.

Failed Water Inlet Valve

The most common cause. Valve stops opening to let water in. Ice production stops completely.

✓ Quick same-day replacement

Frozen Water Line

Arizona temperature swings can freeze the supply line. Easy fix — thawed and insulated same visit.

✓ Fixed same day

Faulty Ice Maker Module

The control module that cycles ice production fails. Replaced with OEM Sub-Zero part same visit.

✓ OEM part replacement

Clogged Water Filter

Arizona hard water clogs filters fast. Slow or no ice production. Easy filter swap.

✓ Filter replacement same day

Broken Ice Ejector

Ice forms but won't eject — stuck arm or broken motor. Repaired or replaced on-site.

✓ Same-day repair

Temperature Too High

Freezer not cold enough for ice to form. Usually related to a larger cooling issue we fix same day.

✓ Diagnosed and fixed
